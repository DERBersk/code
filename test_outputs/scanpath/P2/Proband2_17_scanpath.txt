One person is lying down on a blue paddleboard, while the other is sitting up on a white and blue paddleboard. Several large sharks are visible in the water, swimming around the two boards.

Finally, the user's attention is drawn to another shark swimming to the right of the paddleboards. This indicates that the surprising and potentially dangerous context provided by the sharks is the most engaging and compelling aspect of the image.
